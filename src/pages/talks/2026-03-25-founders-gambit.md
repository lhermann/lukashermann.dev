In this episode of The Founder's Gambit, Gabriel and I walk through the actual steps of building a SaaS — no fluff. We cover how I validated Stagetimer, what a real MVP looks like (hint: not 15 features), how to find users who stick around, and what comes after the first paying customers: reducing churn, SEO, and marketing that compounds.

## Topics Covered

- **Validating before building** - Why "if you don't know a person who would immediately use it, you already built the wrong thing"
- **What an MVP actually is** - People say MVP and then ship 15 features; that's not an MVP
- **From MVP to a proper product** - Rebuilding Stagetimer properly once validation was in
- **Getting users that stay** - Word of mouth, happy users, and retention over acquisition
- **Reducing churn** - Building exactly the one thing a niche needs
- **Growth** - SEO, marketing, and using AI as a developer in 2026
